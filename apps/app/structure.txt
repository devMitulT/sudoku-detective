# Game Concept

Sudoku Detective is a single-player offline mystery board game.

The core gameplay loop is:

Crime Scene
→ Witness Interview
→ Forensics Lab
→ CCTV Investigation
→ Case Analysis
→ Courtroom Accusation

Every completed Sudoku puzzle reveals clues.

Players gradually eliminate suspects and solve the mystery.

The goal is to make Sudoku feel like an investigation rather than a puzzle app.
